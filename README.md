# E-Commerce Sales Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an **E-Commerce Sales Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Dashboard** built in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product trends, payment methods, referral channels, and order status performance.

The dashboard provides interactive business insights through KPI cards, charts, slicers, and pivot table analysis to support data-driven decision-making.

# Dashboard Insights

## Product Performance Analysis

* Chairs generated the highest revenue among all product categories.
* Printers and Laptops also contributed significantly to total sales.
* Phones recorded the lowest revenue despite moderate sales quantity.

## Monthly Sales Trend

* Revenue peaked in June with the highest monthly sales.
* Sales declined between July and September before stabilizing toward year-end.
* Seasonal fluctuations were observed across the reporting period.

## Order Status Analysis

* Delivered and shipped orders accounted for the majority of transactions.
* Returned and cancelled orders remained relatively high and require operational improvements.

## Payment Method Analysis

* Online and Credit Card payments were the most preferred payment methods.
* Gift Card transactions recorded the lowest customer usage.

## Referral Source Analysis

* Instagram generated the highest number of customer orders.
* Referral and Facebook channels produced strong revenue performance.
* Customer acquisition was well distributed across multiple traffic sources.

# Recommendations

1. Increase promotions for high-performing products such as Chairs and Printers.

2. Improve customer service and delivery operations to reduce cancelled and returned orders.

3. Launch targeted marketing campaigns during lower-performing months.

4. Improve visibility and marketing strategies for underperforming products like Phones.

5. Continue strengthening digital payment systems and online customer engagement.
